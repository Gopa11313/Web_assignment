More About The Club

Diamond Gym club was established in 2010 with a motive of providing easily accessible gym along with a lot of fitness to the locals. In the market Diamond gym club is the most complete gym club. The activities we have going in gym are namely Strength Training, Aardio, Aerobics, Zumba and many more. We provide facilities like shower after gym, parking, saftey locker for each client who has a six months membership. The prices of packages have been set analyzing financial capabilities of the locality so the prices are quite fair and resonnable. If you want to join swimming or steam bath then the payment of this facilities will be include in the final bill. You can also hire a personal trainer to better guide you with your training.
